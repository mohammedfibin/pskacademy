Who We Are

Founded in 2025 in Malappuram, Kerala, PSK Academy (Practical • Skill • Knowledge) is committed to shaping confident, capable, and future-ready individuals.

We specialise in practical training that strengthens essential skills including Personality Development, Public Speaking, and Business Skills.

Our programs focus on real-world application through interactive, activity-based sessions that help learners communicate effectively, think clearly, and perform with confidence.

With a mission to empower individuals with lifelong skills, PSK Academy aims to be Malappuram’s most trusted destination for personal and professional growth.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical communication and life skills through activity-based training.

Our Vision

To become Kerala’s most trusted academy for personal and professional transformation.
